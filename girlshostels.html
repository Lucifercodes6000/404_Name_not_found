Girls Hostel Facilities

Hostel Types

There are a total of 8 women's hostels in VIT (air-conditioned and non-air-conditioned). The G and H block is an apartment-style hostel where each wing has its own separate washroom. The G and H blocks have non-apartment style rooms as well.The blocks allotted to freshers change periodically.The C D E F blcoks are normal type rooms and have bunk beds with non attached washrooms. A nd b Blocks are non air-conditioned.

Laundry

Laundry facility is available on each block on the ground first and second floors each floor of each block is assigned to a particular floor laundry .You need to give your laundry over there only .You can submit 25 clothes without bedsheet or 10 clothes and 1 bedsheet at a time. Also you have been provided 44 washes free included in your hostel fees.
